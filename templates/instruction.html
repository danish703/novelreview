Do you want to know how GeNovel works

Here are the instructions while using GeNovel: Genuine Novel Review

  • Choose the genre for you ease to view novels.
  • You can see basic information about the novels.
  • Click novel of you choice to read more about it.
  • Know what other people think about this novel from their review, rating and a polarity generated as a sentiment value based on the review.
  • Read this book? Then login from your account to post a comment. If you are new, sign up a new account.
