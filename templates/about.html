About GeNovel: Genuine Novel Review

Welcome to GeNovel

Genuine Novel Review Web Application, a great eye for good books.

Using the text analysis feature from opinion mining technique, your reviews will automatically be polarized as positive, negative or neutral sentiment value. If you want to read best novels as per the genuine reviews of people, you are using the right novel review application. The reviews are being managed by our administrator that depicts storing only accurate and relevant reviews on novels. Hurry up and sign up to use our application. Your credential information are safe with us. Feel free to provide your feedbacks. We are delighted if we have to add more genres and novels as per the need. So stay connected with us.





Aim:

A novel review-based web application for the purpose of posting and accessing genuine reviews on different novel books easily by users. This leads to a time efficient, user friendly and well-managed web application.



Mission:

1. To improve customer experience and reduce time consumption by separating genres of novel books into different categories.
2. To authorize registered users to post product reviews and ratings to novel books through their own account.
3. To monitor product reviews with ratings by presenting the genuine rating sentiment score using opinion mining technique.
4. To increase accuracy of reviews by ability of administrator to delete unnecessary reviews posted about novels.
